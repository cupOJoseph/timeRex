Features

Launch your own time token. Free

Easily launch a token on the Ethereum blockchain that represents your time. We don't take a percentage or any fee for launching a token

Explore featured users

Top users who we trust and have proven themselves are featured on our site, and can sell their tokens to our audience.

Store and save others time

Freelancers often get booked up. Buying their tokens (and communicating with them) gives you the chance to buy their time whenever and work with them later when your project is ready for their help.

Create a market for your time

Easily add your ERC-20 compliant token to Uniswap and create a market for your time where the price is set by demand.

Trade hours between people

Developers and designers can trade hours working on each others projects. Or better, bring different kinds of people together at once for your ideas.

Stake time

Make event goers buy the time you spent planning an event, and give it back if they show up.
