TimeRex is a personal token management dapp. A personal tokens is any token issued by an individual who makes a promise to do some task or spend some amount of time. In our case we say each token represents 1 hour of the issuers time, although individuals are free to use it how they want.

We think personal tokens are cool because:

  • You can Invest in individuals
  • Top freelancers who are busy can sell their future time to people who want to schedule their project into tight schedules
  • We can create open markets to trade time with each other, and individuals can be paid more fairly for their labour
  • Helpers can donate their time and skills, or those of others, directly to charity and open source projects they want to support.
  • Coming Soon right here: Creating bundles of different users' time to build new teams of devs, designers, project managers, anything else, all thrown together for maximum effectiveness.
